Frequently Asked Questions

How does the auto-reply feature work?
Our auto-reply system uses keyword detection to send appropriate responses instantly. You can set up custom templates and rules for different types of inquiries, ensuring accurate and quick responses 24/7.
Is bulk messaging allowed on WhatsApp?
Yes, but with proper guidelines. Our system ensures compliance with WhatsApp's business policies, preventing spam flags while allowing you to send personalized bulk messages to your opted-in contacts.
How secure is the WhatsApp Bot?
Our bot operates with end-to-end encryption and follows WhatsApp's security protocols. Your data and conversations are fully secured and comply with privacy regulations including GDPR.
Can I customize the bot's responses?
Absolutely! You can create custom response templates, set up specific keywords, and personalize messages. The bot can be trained to handle various scenarios based on your business needs.
What analytics are available?
Our dashboard provides comprehensive analytics including message delivery rates, response times, customer engagement metrics, popular inquiries, and conversion tracking. You can export reports for detailed analysis.
How do I get started?
Getting started is easy! Sign up for an account, connect your WhatsApp Business API, set up your initial responses, and you're ready to go. Our team provides full support during the setup process.
